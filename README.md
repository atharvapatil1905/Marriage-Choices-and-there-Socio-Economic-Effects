## **Project Objectives**

* Analyse how age and financial independence shift across life stages
* Evaluate the importance of financial stability in marriage decisions
* Understand preferred family size and preparedness for raising children
* Compare marriage attitudes in urban vs rural areas
* Study views on prenuptial agreements, debt, and shared finances

## **Summary of Key Insights**

* **25–30** years is seen as the ideal marriage age
* Strong support for **equal (50–50) financial contribution**
* Majority prefer **simple/court weddings** over lavish ones
* Emotional companionship is the **top reason** for marriage
* Large support for **prenuptial agreements**
* Financial independence strongly influences marriage decisions
